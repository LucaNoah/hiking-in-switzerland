Discover Switzerland ...

in the winter

Icy glaciers, snow-covered mountains and fairy-tale sights. Experience unforgettable moments during extended snowshoe hikes in the midst of a breathtaking mountain world.

safely guided

Out and about, across Switzerland, up and down, together with us. Discover the unknown safely and guided. Free of charge and beginner friendly.

in the summer

Pure nature, grass-green landscapes as they exist only in Switzerland. Bathing in a mountain lake or spending the night under the stars, the highest pleasure during long-distance hikes in the Alps.
